## Key Takeaways

1. **mirraw** (marketplace) has the highest feature density — massive catalog, rich tools, price alerts
2. **utsavFashion** leads among D2C brands — 42 features including loyalty, stitching, international infrastructure
3. **suta** is feature-light but has strongest content marketing + brand differentiation
4. **houseofraadhvi** is the most basic — minimal features, relies on discount-driven conversions
5. **pothys** and **nalli** leverage physical retail trust for online credibility
6. **karagiri** differentiates through combo bundles + best seller tagging
7. Common gaps across all: No AR/VR try-on, no PWA, limited video/drape guides
