## Selling Strategy
- **Traditional Trust**: Well-known South Indian retail chain — physical store trust drives online
- **Free Shipping**: On all orders (no minimum — strong differentiator)
- **COD**: Available — critical for Indian market
- **Discounting**: Moderate (20–50% off), category-wide seasonal sales
- **Regional Specialization**: Deep catalog in Kanchipuram silk, Banarasi, Cotton — regional strength
- **Inventory Breadth**: Large catalog with extensive stock per category
- **Omnichannel**: Multiple physical stores + online — click & collect possible
- **Festival Marketing**: Heavy promotions during Pongal, Diwali, Wedding season
- **Social Proof**: "Best Seller" tags, reviews/ratings
- **Returns**: Easy returns policy
- **NRI Focus**: International shipping with country-specific handling

## Strengths
- Strong regional brand trust (South Indian family favorite for generations)
- Very large catalog across silk + cotton varieties
- Excellent regional specialization (Kanchipuram, Banarasi)
- Omnichannel presence (physical stores for trust + pickup)
- Free shipping on all orders (no threshold)
- COD + easy returns — low purchase friction
- Good SEO for traditional saree searches

## Weaknesses / Gaps
- Website design is traditional/dated (not modern UX)
- Mobile experience is functional but not premium
- No live chat or WhatsApp support
- No video/drape guide
- No AR/VR try-on
- No blog or content marketing visible
- No loyalty/rewards program
- No combo bundles promoted
- No blouse stitching service integrated
- No first-order discount popup observed
- Navigation can be overwhelming (too many categories)

## Unique Differentiators
- "Pothys" brand name is synonymous with silk sarees in South India
- Deep physical retail network in Tamil Nadu
- Specialization in Kanchipuram silk with region-specific catalog
- Free shipping with no minimum threshold
- Weight mentioned for sarees (quality indicator for silk)
- Region-based saree taxonomy (Kanchipuram, Banarasi, Pochampally)
