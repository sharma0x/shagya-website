## Selling Strategy
- **Storytelling-Driven**: Every product has a name, story, and heritage behind it — key differentiator
- **Premium Pricing**: High AOV — minimal discounting, focuses on brand value
- **First Order Discount**: 10% off for newsletter signup
- **Free Shipping**: Prepaid orders only (encourages digital payment)
- **Content Marketing**: "Suta Stories" blog — weaver stories, design inspiration, styling tips
- **Social Proof**: Press logos (Vogue, Elle) + Instagram feed embedded
- **Community Building**: Strong Instagram community, user-generated content
- **Limited Drops**: New collection drops create urgency (not heavy discounting)
- **Sustainability Angle**: "Handloom Revival", "Sustainable Fashion" — appeals to eco-conscious buyer
- **Mobile-First**: Dedicated app for iOS + Android
- **Brand Experience**: Minimalist, editorial aesthetic — product as art

## Strengths
- Strong, differentiated brand identity (modern handloom)
- Excellent content marketing (Suta Stories blog)
- Premium pricing with no race-to-the-bottom discounts
- Very strong Instagram presence + community
- Clean, modern UX — mobile responsive
- Press credibility (Vogue, Elle, Femina)
- Fabric + weaver storytelling adds emotional value

## Weaknesses / Gaps
- No reviews/ratings on homepage listings (brand relies on Instagram for social proof)
- No live chat or WhatsApp support visible
- No video/drape guide on listings
- No AR/VR try-on
- No loyalty/rewards program
- No bundled combos (saree + blouse + accessories)
- No store pickup (online-only)
- No COD for larger orders (prepaid-only focus)
- Returns policy not prominently featured
- Catalog is smaller than competitors (curated, not exhaustive)

## Unique Differentiators
- Weave/fabric storytelling per product
- Press partnerships (Vogue, Elle)
- Strong editorial/blog content
- Mobile app presence
- Sustainability + handloom revival mission-driven brand
- Minimal discounting — brand value driven pricing
