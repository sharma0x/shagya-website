# utsavfashion.com — Research Document

## Overview
- **URL**: https://www.utsavfashion.com
- **Platform**: Magento (inferred from URL structure: .html suffixes, /customer/account/, /catalog/ paths)
- **Segment**: Global Indian ethnic wear — sarees, salwar kameez, lehengas, kurta sets
- **Target Audience**: NRI women worldwide, Indian diaspora, ages 25–60
- **Established**: ~2003 (well-established brand)

## Site Architecture & Navigation
- **Top Nav**: Logo (left) → department categories (right): Sarees, Salwar Kameez, Lehengas, Kurta Sets, Jewellery, Fabric, Accessories
- **Mega-menu**: Rich dropdown on hover with subcategories by fabric, type, occasion, price
- **Secondary bar**: Search (prominent), Account, Wishlist, Cart
- **Utility bar (top-most)**: Free Shipping Worldwide, Easy Returns, Trusted since 2003, Customer Care
- **Breadcrumbs**: Present on all internal pages
- **Footer**: Extensive — categories, policies, customer service, about us, social media, newsletter, payment methods, international shipping info

- Care Instructions
- Availability / In Stock indicator

## Selling Strategy
- **First Order Discount**: 10% off for newsletter signup — prominent popup on entry
- **Free Shipping**: Worldwide free shipping above certain threshold
- **Global Focus**: Currency selector, country-specific shipping info, international returns
- **Discounting**: Seasonal sales (wedding season, Diwali, End of Season), category-wide discounts
- **Urgency**: Low stock indicators on PDP
- **Social Proof**: Reviews and ratings visible
- **Cross-sell**: "Complete the Look" — matching jewellery, accessories on PDP
- **Bundles**: Saree + blouse stitching service as add-on
- **Loyalty**: Reward points on purchases
- **Email/SMS**: Cart abandonment emails, browse abandonment, back-in-stock alerts
- **Content Marketing**: Blog with saree draping guides, occasion styling, fabric care
- **Trust Signals**: "Trusted since 2003", secure checkout badges, money-back guarantee
- **Chat**: Live chat widget for instant support

## Strengths
- Very comprehensive feature set — best-in-class among Indian ethnic wear sites
- Excellent international shipping setup with country-specific handling
- Rich product filtering makes navigation easy for large catalog
- Blog + content marketing drives organic traffic
- Multiple payment options including international
- Strong trust signals (established brand, reviews, secure badges)

## Weaknesses / Gaps
- Old Magento platform can feel slower than modern Shopify sites
- Design feels dated compared to newer D2C brands
- Mobile experience is functional but not as polished as competitors
- Average product imagery quality (varies by collection)
- No AR/VR try-on feature
- Returns for international orders are complex
- Blog content is irregularly updated

## Unique Differentiators vs Competitors
- Global shipping infrastructure better than most Indian saree sites
- Currency converter + country-specific pricing
- Price Match Guarantee
- Bridal appointment booking
- Bulk/corporate ordering
