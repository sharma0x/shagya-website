# nalli.com — Research Document

## Overview
- **URL**: https://www.nalli.com
- **Platform**: Shopify (inferred from /collections/, /products/, /cart URL patterns)
- **Segment**: Heritage silk sarees (est. 1928), premium traditional wear
- **Target Audience**: Women aged 30–65, South Indian (esp. Tamil), cultural/event shoppers, NRIs
- **Established**: 1928 (97 years of heritage)

## Selling Strategy
- **Heritage Positioning**: "Elegance since 1928" — legacy and trust as primary brand asset
- **First Order Discount**: 5% off for newsletter signup
- **Free Shipping**: Above ₹999 domestic
- **Returns**: 14-day easy return policy
- **Discounting**: Seasonal sales (Pongal, Diwali, Wedding Season), clearances, category discounts
- **Festival Marketing**: Strong festival season promotions in hero banners
- **Cross-sell**: Matching blouses, jewellery on PDP (inferred from standard Shopify setup)
- **Urgency**: Limited stock / limited period offers on sale items
- **Physical + Digital**: 20+ stores complement online — click & collect likely
- **NRI Focus**: "We ship worldwide" messaging, international payment options

## Strengths
- Strong brand heritage (est. 1928) builds immediate trust
- Large catalog with deep inventory across fabrics and regions
- Omnichannel presence (20+ physical stores + online)
- Good SEO (long-established domain, many product pages indexed)
- Trustworthy with clear policies and easy returns
- Festival-specific collections drive seasonal traffic

## Weaknesses / Gaps
- Design feels dated (traditional, not modern UX)
- Mobile experience is functional but not premium
- Limited content marketing (blog/spread not visible)
- No video/drape guide on listing
- No AR/VR try-on
- No blouse stitching service integrated online (inferred)
- No loyalty/rewards program visible
- No WhatsApp/live chat support
- No bundles or combo offers promoted

## Unique Differentiators
- 97-year heritage brand unmatched by competitors
- Physical store network for trust + omnichannel
- Deep Kanchipuram silk catalog (regional specialty)
- Strong South Indian customer base
