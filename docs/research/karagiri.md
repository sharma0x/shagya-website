## Selling Strategy
- **Combo Strategy**: Strong focus on "Combo Offers" — saree + blouse combos at bundled prices
- **Discounting**: Moderate discounting (20–50% off), visible discount badges
- **Free Shipping**: On all orders (no minimum threshold — strong conversion driver)
- **COD**: Available (broadens trust for new customers)
- **Categorization by Fabric**: Rich fabric taxonomy helps fabric-preference shoppers
- **Cross-sell**: Bundles and combos increase AOV
- **Content Marketing**: Blog for SEO + engagement
- **Social Proof**: Instagram feed + "Best Seller" tags build trust
- **Returns**: Easy returns policy
- **Occasion-Based Selling**: Wedding, Festive, Party, Daily Wear sections

## Strengths
- Clean, modern Shopify store with good UX
- Combo offer strategy is effective for increasing AOV
- Rich fabric-based categorization aids navigation
- "Best Seller" badges provide social proof
- Easy returns + COD + free shipping = low friction
- Good mobile responsiveness
- Instagram integration adds social proof

## Weaknesses / Gaps
- No reviews/ratings visible on listing/PDP (major trust gap)
- No live chat or WhatsApp support
- No video/drape guide
- No AR/VR try-on
- No loyalty/rewards program
- No blog prominently featured (may exist but not highlighted)
- No store pickup / store locator (online only)
- No first-order discount popup observed
- Saree-specific content (styling, care) minimal

## Unique Differentiators
- Combo offers (saree + blouse pre-bundled) — better than most competitors
- "Best Seller" tagging on popular products
- Strong fabric-based navigation taxonomy
- Free shipping with no minimum threshold
