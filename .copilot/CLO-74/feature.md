# CLO-74: Customer reviews with photos on product detail page

## Overview

Reviews were only visible on the homepage as testimonials. Added a dedicated review section to the PDP showing customer-uploaded photos, star ratings, verified purchase badges, and review text.

## Features

- Average rating summary (e.g., "4.7 · 12 reviews")
- Customer photo/avatar + name + rating + date
- "Verified Purchase" badge
- Review photos as clickable thumbnails (expand to full view)
- Empty state: "No reviews yet"
- Fetches only approved reviews for the current product
